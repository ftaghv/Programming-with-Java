Course Logistics and Policies

Grading

Homeworks Students will complete one homework assignment after each class session, for a total of 11 assignments. Each homework will focus on the concepts covered that day and will include both theoretical questions and hands-on coding exercises.

Exam Dates
  • Final: Friday, July 18th from 9:15-11:00 a.m.

Exam Notes Sheet The exam is open book and open note. You can use anything we've covered in class, but you are not allowed to use any AI tools. The exam is meant to check your own understanding of the material.
